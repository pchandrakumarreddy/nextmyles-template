how it works

We make it easy for shippers and carriers to get connected. Available loads are automatically matched with vetted carriers based on geographical preferences and predictive load offering technology for delivery as fast and efficiently as possible. Real-time GPS tracks the status of the shipment throughout the entire trip and the end-to-end process, including invoicing and billing, is handled within the NEXT online marketplace and mobile app.

2
